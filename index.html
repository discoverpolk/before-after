About this Project

Sustainable Frenchtown and the Hunterdon County Creative Team are conducting an inventory of all things creative in and around the Frenchtown area. Knowing what we have, who we are, and where we work and play is the first step in building a creative network.

The success of this project depends on YOU! A comprehensive inventory and map of our assets is the first step to advancing and developing the arts in our area. Add your assets, spread the word, and get involved! Contact us at creativeteam.assets@gmail.com.

Our Team

Sustainable Frenchtown

Sustainable Frenchtown's mission is to help Frenchtown be the most viable, thriving community we can be. Frenchtown is a Sustainable Jersey certified community--one of only 205 in the state. Meetings are open to all, and take place on the 2nd Tuesday of each month at 8 pm at Borough Hall. Connect with us here.

The Creative Team

The Hunterdon County Creative Team was created in 2016 as a way of connecting people who care about the arts and creativity in our county, and the ways that fostering creativity makes our communities more sustainable.

Environmental Connection, Inc.

Environmental Connection works in sustainability and community redevelopment, and has a deep familiarity with the Sustainable Jersey program. Project consultants Susan Mania and Iana Dikidjieva have considerable experience between them in the arts, community-engaged asset mapping, and the creative community in Hunterdon County.
